Novenas is an application dedicated to spirituality, offering novenas, liturgies, and daily prayers. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your information. This Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Novenas does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email, or precise location.
We only collect:

Important: We do not collect sensitive, religious, or financial data.

The information collected is used to:
